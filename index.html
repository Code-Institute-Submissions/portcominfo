About Portarlington

  1. Home
  2. About

Portarlington, in Irish: Cúil an tSúdaire, meaning "nook of the tanner", is a town on the border of County Laois and County Offaly, Ireland. The River Barrow forms the border. The town was recorded in the 2016 census as having a population of 8,368.Wikipedia
