Welcome to PortInfo-2021.

We’re here to provide useful information about the town of Portarlington (in Irish, Cúil an tSúdaire, which means “nook of the tanner”, a town which straddles the River Barrow, and is on the border of Counties Laois and Offaly, in the Irish midlands.

Whether you have just moved to the town, or are thinking of doing so, information about its facilities is always important. Where are the best places to eat out? Where do you get heating oil or the all-important peat briquettes? For all these answers, look at our Local Info page.
